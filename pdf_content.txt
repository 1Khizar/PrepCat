1. Executive SummaryPrepBuddy is a comprehensive, AI-powered EdTech platform purpose-built for Pakistani students
preparing for MDCAT (Medical and Dental College Admission Test) and NUMS (National University of
Medical Sciences) entrance examinations. Every year, over 200,000 students compete for a limited
number of medical college seats in Pakistan. The preparation journey is hampered by scattered resources,
lack of organized past papers, absence of personalised feedback, and no structured self-assessment
tools.
PrepBuddy solves all of this in one beautifully designed, student-friendly platform. It combines a rich
past-paper bank, a subject-wise and chapter-wise quiz engine, AI-generated explanations powered by
Groq LLM, gamification (streaks, leaderboards, friend competitions), real-time performance analytics, and
a news feed — delivering everything a student needs to crack MDCAT or NUMS in one place.

2. Problem Statement
1. Scattered Past Papers
Students spend hours searching across WhatsApp groups, Telegram channels, and random websites for
past papers. There is no single, verified, well-organised repository for MDCAT/NUMS past papers.
2. No Personalised Practice
Generic YouTube videos and PDF books do not adapt to a student's weak areas. There is no tool that
says: 'You are weak in Genetics — here are targeted MCQs.'
3. Zero Progress Tracking
Students have no way to measure their improvement over time. They do not know their accuracy per
subject, speed per question, or readiness level before the exam.
4. No Motivation Mechanism
Preparation for 1–2 years is mentally exhausting. Without streaks, leaderboards, or friend competitions,
students lose focus and motivation easily.
5. Lack of Instant Explanations
When a student gets an MCQ wrong, they often have no immediate, clear explanation of the correct
answer with scientific reasoning — forcing them to search elsewhere.
6. No Real-Time MDCAT News
Policy changes, schedule updates, and seat allocation news are critical — yet there is no single trusted
place where students get MDCAT/NUMS news alongside their study tools.

13. Pricing Strategy & Business ModelPrepBuddy's business model is built for long-term dominance — not short-term revenue. The strategy is
simple: Launch completely free, build a loyal student base, become the go-to name for MDCAT prep in
Pakistan, and then introduce paid tiers once students cannot imagine preparing without PrepBuddy. This
is exactly how Duolingo, Notion, and every successful EdTech platform has won their markets.
■ Launch Philosophy: FREE FIRST, MONETISE LATER
When PrepBuddy launches, 100% of all features are completely free for every student. No
credit card, no trial limits, no paywalls. Every MCQ, every past paper, every AI explanation,
every streak, every friend challenge — all free. The goal in Phase 1 and 2 is user
acquisition and trust, not revenue. Once PrepBuddy has 10,000+ active students and
strong word-of-mouth, the premium tier activates in Phase 3. Students who joined free are
grandfathered with generous free access — they become the biggest advocates of the
platform.

13.5 Why 'Free First' is the Right Strategy for Pakistan
 Pakistani students are extremely price-sensitive. Asking for money at signup kills conversion instantly
— even PKR 200 is a barrier.
 Word-of-mouth is the #1 growth channel in Pakistan. A student who loves PrepBuddy tells 10 friends.
Free removes all friction to try it.
 Building a large free user base creates a powerful data moat — PrepBuddy knows more about
MDCAT prep patterns than anyone else.
 Once students have months of streak history, quiz data, and friends on PrepBuddy, switching to a
competitor becomes painful. Retention becomes natural.
 Investors, media, and academies take PrepBuddy more seriously with 50,000 users than with 500
paying users. Free growth builds credibility.
